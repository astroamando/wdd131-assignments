Productivity Tips

1. The Pomodoro Technique

Work for 25 minutes, then take a 5-minute break. This keeps your brain fresh and focused.

2. Plan the Night Before

Spend 10 minutes each night listing your top 3 tasks for the next day.

3. Prioritize Sleep

A tired brain cannot focus. Ensure you get 7-8 hours of sleep for maximum cognitive function.
